Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

BiomeAndMind ("we," "our," or "us") is a wellness tracking application designed to help users observe patterns related to food, mood, energy, and lifestyle. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

BiomeAndMind is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Any insights provided are for informational and wellness tracking purposes only.

1. Information We Collect

BiomeAndMind collects the following types of information:

  • Health and wellness data you voluntarily provide (self-reported), including food intake, probiotic consumption, mood scores, energy levels, sleep patterns, and supplement usage
  • Usage data such as app features used and session frequency (anonymized)
  • Device information necessary for app functionality and crash reporting
  • Subscription information processed securely through Apple's App Store

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Calculate your Gut-Brain Score and generate AI-assisted insights based on patterns in your data
  • Generate your weekly AI wellness report
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Process your subscription through Apple's secure payment system

3. AI Processing

BiomeAndMind uses third-party AI services (such as Anthropic's Claude) to generate insights based on your data. Data sent for processing is anonymized where possible and transmitted securely. We do not control how third-party AI providers may process data once received, but we take reasonable steps to minimize exposure and protect your information.

4. Data Storage

Your health and wellness data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. We do not maintain centralized long-term storage of your personal health data beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data; however, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Health Data Disclaimer

BiomeAndMind is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. The information provided is for general wellness tracking purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making changes to your diet or health routine.

11. HealthKit
