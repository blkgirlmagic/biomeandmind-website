Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

BiomeAndMind ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

BiomeAndMind collects the following types of information:

  • Health and wellness data you voluntarily enter, including food intake, probiotic consumption, mood scores, energy levels, and sleep hours
  • Usage data such as app features used and session frequency (anonymized)
  • Device information necessary for app functionality and crash reporting
  • Subscription information processed securely through Apple's App Store

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Calculate your Gut-Brain Score and provide personalized AI insights
  • Generate your weekly health report
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Process your subscription through Apple's secure payment system

3. AI Processing

BiomeAndMind uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate personalized health insights. Your health data is sent to our secure Cloudflare proxy server, which forwards anonymized data to Anthropic's API. We do not store your data on Anthropic's servers. All AI processing occurs through encrypted connections.

4. Data Storage

Your health data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Health Data Disclaimer

BiomeAndMind is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. The information provided is for general wellness tracking purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making changes to your diet or health routine.

6. HealthKit

With your permission, BiomeAndMind may access Apple HealthKit data to enhance your gut-brain tracking. HealthKit data is used only within the app and is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through Settings → Reset All Data within the app. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

8. Children's Privacy

BiomeAndMind is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page.
